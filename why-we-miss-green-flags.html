Why We Miss Green Flags

Your nervous system isn’t crazy, bestie—it’s just trained for chaos. If you grew up equating unpredictability or inconsistency with love, green flags might feel boring or even suspicious. Let’s reframe that.

Consistency, clarity, and calm? Those are the real green flags. They might not trigger the adrenaline rush you’ve mistaken for connection, but they are what safe love actually feels like. No more confusing drama for desire. You deserve peace *and* passion—at the same time.
