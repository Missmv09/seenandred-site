About Seen and Red

We're your AI-powered bestie with a PhD in pattern recognition. Our mission is to help you decode confusing messages, recognize relationship patterns, and trust your intuition.

Using advanced AI technology, we analyze your conversations to identify red flags, green flags, and the patterns that might be keeping you stuck in confusion.

Whether you're missing green flags because of past trauma or ignoring red flags because manipulation feels familiar, we're here to help you see clearly and break the cycle.

Important Disclaimer

We are not mental health therapists, counselors, or medical professionals. Our AI-powered analysis is for educational and informational purposes only. The insights provided should not replace professional therapy, medical advice, or crisis intervention.

If you're experiencing domestic violence, mental health crisis, or need professional therapeutic support, please contact:

  • National Domestic Violence Hotline: 1-800-799-7233
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988

Our service is designed to complement, not replace, professional mental health care. For serious relationship concerns, trauma processing, or ongoing mental health support, please consult with licensed professionals.
