Mixed Signals Aren’t a Mystery

If he’s hot one day, cold the next—it’s not deep. He’s not confused. He knows exactly what he’s doing. Mixed signals are a strategy to keep you guessing and chasing. That back-and-forth? That’s the hook.

Don’t decode what’s inconsistent. Don’t romanticize crumbs. If someone wanted to be with you, you’d know. Loudly. Consistently. Mixed signals aren’t love, they’re leverage. Step off the rollercoaster—you don’t owe him another ride.
