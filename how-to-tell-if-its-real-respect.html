How to Tell If It’s Real Respect

Respect isn’t just about holding the door—it’s about holding space. If he listens without interrupting, values your time, honors your boundaries without a pouty face—that’s real.

Real respect feels safe. It’s consistent, not performative. It doesn’t disappear when things aren’t going his way. If you're always second-guessing your needs to keep the peace, that’s not respect—it’s walking on eggshells. You weren’t born to shrink. Period.
