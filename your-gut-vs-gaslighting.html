Your Gut vs. Gaslighting 🤯

Gaslighting doesn’t always sound like “You’re crazy.” Sometimes it sounds like “You're too sensitive” or “I never said that.”

If you constantly second-guess yourself around someone, that’s not intuition — it’s erosion of trust in your own reality.

Trust your gut — it's the part of you that remembers what your brain tries to rationalize.
