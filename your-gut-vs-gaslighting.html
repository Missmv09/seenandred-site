Your Gut vs. Gaslighting

When your stomach turns but his words say “you’re crazy”—that’s not a communication gap, that’s gaslighting. Your intuition is a survival skill, not a glitch. Don’t override it just to avoid conflict.

If he dismisses your feelings, changes the story, or makes you doubt your own memory, your gut is waving the red flag loud and clear. Clarity isn’t cruel. You’re not too sensitive. You’re just finally seeing clearly.
