Is It Gaslighting or Just Miscommunication?

by Red 💋

Let’s get into it, bestie. Gaslighting is not just someone disagreeing with you — it’s when they try to make you question your reality. Think: “That never happened,” when it absolutely did. According to Psychology Today, gaslighting is a form of psychological manipulation where someone causes you to doubt your perception of events, memories, or even your sanity.

💡 Tip: One-off confusion is normal. Repeated denial of clear events? 🚩 That’s manipulation.

If a partner’s version of reality always centers them as the victim and you as “crazy,” it’s time to take a step back and run a decode. 👀
