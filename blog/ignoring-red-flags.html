Ignoring Red Flags

Published: August 8, 2024 • ~6 min read

Red flags don’t vanish; they compound. Here’s a quick rubric to spot, test, and decide without self‑gaslighting.

Common Red Flags

  • Hot–cold attention, especially after intimacy.
  • Secretive phone habits + defensiveness when asked.
  • Words of commitment; actions of casual.

Test the Behaviour

  1. State a boundary (“I don’t continue when communication drops”).
  2. Offer one chance to repair with a clear request.
  3. If repeated—end contact kindly and completely.

Self‑Protection

  • Tell a friend your plan; ask them to mirror you.
  • Block instead of monitoring.
  • Journal what you did right, not just what hurt.
