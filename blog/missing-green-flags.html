Missing Green Flags

Published: July 30, 2024 • ~5 min read

When chaos feels like chemistry, steady can feel “too easy.” Use this to recalibrate what healthy looks like.

The Big Three Greens

  • Consistency: plans, follow‑through, repair after conflict.
  • Emotional safety: you can say no, ask questions, and feel heard.
  • Reciprocity: effort and care flow both directions.

Micro‑tests

  • Ask for a small change; see if it sticks.
  • Share a boundary; watch for defensiveness vs. curiosity.
  • Miss a text on purpose; do they spiral or stay steady?
