Dating in Your 20s: The Swipe Decade

Situationships, rosters, and clarity in the swipe era.

Dating apps turned the hunt for connection into a thumb workout. Options feel endless, but so does the confusion. Here's how twenty‑somethings can move through the swipe decade with their sanity intact.

Situationships

The “what are we?” limbo is a favorite in this decade. If you find yourself on the edge of something more, ask for clarity early. Your time isn’t a trial period; it’s an investment.

Rosters

Keeping a lineup can feel safe, but juggling hearts is exhausting. If you’re dating multiple people, be transparent and check your motives—are you exploring or avoiding?

Clarity Over Chaos

Whether it’s a chatty match or a slow burn, set simple boundaries: how often you talk, how soon you meet, what you want. Clear is kind, even when feelings are casual.

Swipe with intention, show up with integrity, and remember—the goal isn’t more matches, it’s a better match.
